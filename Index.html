Hello everyone! We have updates to share, please make sure to read everything!

First of all, thank you for believing in the project and deciding to have fun and play with us! We have reached almost 100 unique accounts created!

Server Updates

The server is going to be updated today and will include the following changes:

  • Founder system implementation: To thank all of our early players, everyone who has played until now and everyone who plays until the 25th of August (included) will be forever recognized as a Founder. What does this mean? Your account will be flagged as a Founder, meaning that forever, every new character you create will automatically receive the [Founder] title and a [Pink Poo] mascot sent by Mail (check your System Mail tab!) by Arale itself! So make sure to spread the word to family, friends, and strangers so everyone can benefit from this once-in-a-lifetime reward! Note: Existing characters will also receive both rewards, don't worry!
  • Pet Fusion fixes: You can now fuse pets without using any items, with a 70% success rate. Using an item will guarantee a successful fusion.
  • Animation fixes: Previously, monsters were frozen between each attack animation; now they will properly idle between attacks. Also, player attack animations will feel smoother now.
  • Knockdown / Slip mechanics: If you managed to finish the combo of all normal attack animations (6 in total, unlocked at level 20), you'd have a 100% chance to either knock down the enemy or make it slip. This is implemented now.

Client Updates

The client has also received some updates, so please make sure to go to the account panel on the website and download them!

  • More localization fixes:
    • Identified the real cause of the crashes related to reflected damage. They have been correctly fixed, and the reflected damage will properly show in the combat log now. Unless you update your client, you will crash again, so please update it as soon as possible!
    • Improvements to early Human quests and random dialogs around the world.
    • Improved some UI elements.

Additional Updates

We also enabled more commands for players. This is the current list:

  • @online: Shows the number of currently online players.
  • @addmasteritem: Adds the item used to complete the master quest for your class, in case you lose it by accident. Can only be used after you accept it from Korin.
  • @addskill2: Unlocks the master class passive skill, in case a bug causes you to lose it. Can only be used after you have unlocked your master class.
  • @addhtb: Unlocks the main HTB and master class HTB, if you have already learned them. This is meant to be used after a skill reset. Can only be used after you have unlocked your master class.
  • @unstuck: Teleports you back to your Popo Stone if you are stuck.
  • @resetexp: Resets your level experience to 0.
  • @exp (on/off): Toggles whether your character continues to gain experience or not. It is used to continue farming without gaining any more levels, to gather gear for kid budokai, for example.
